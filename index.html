Our Story

A team built on empathy and love

Bentley is a friendly, empathic Italian Greyhound with a natural gift for keeping other pets company. Whether your furry friend is a dog or a cat, Bentley knows just how to make them feel at ease and never alone.

Behind every great pet sitter is a dedicated human. I'm Emma, and while Bentley provides the warmth and companionship, I make sure your pet is safe, active, and truly happy throughout their stay.

"Bentley keeps the company. I make sure you're both safe, active, and happy."
